## 📅 Tuesday, Apr 21 — Cardio + Core

**Floor rule:** 10 min treadmill walk + 1 set dead bugs

**Warmup:** 5 min brisk treadmill walk

| Exercise | Sets | Notes |
|----------|------|-------|
| Treadmill intervals | 20 min | 2 min walk → 1 min jog/fast walk, repeat. No sprinting — just elevate heart rate. |
| Dead bug | 3×10 each side | Lie on back, arms up. Extend opposite arm + leg toward floor. Lower back stays flat. | 
| Bird dog | 3×10 each side | On hands and knees, extend opposite arm + leg. Slow and controlled. No arching. |
| Glute bridge | 3×15 | Feet flat, push hips up. Squeeze glutes hard at top. Hold 1 sec. |
| Forearm plank | 3×20–30 sec | Hips level, core braced, breathe normally. Stop before form breaks. |

## 📅 Thursday, Apr 23 — Full Body Strength

**Cue:** Set your gym bag by the door the night before
**Floor rule:** 3 goblet squats + 3 dumbbell rows — that's it

**Warmup:** 5 min brisk treadmill walk

| Exercise | Sets | Notes |
|----------|------|-------|
| Goblet squat | 3×12 | Hold dumbbell at chest. Squat slowly, knees track over toes. Drive through heels. |
| Dumbbell chest press | 3×10 | Press straight up, control descent — 2 seconds down. Floor or bench. |
| Romanian deadlift | 3×10 | Hinge at hips, flat back, core tight. Lower to shin level. Stop if lower back complains. |
| Seated shoulder press | 3×10 | Seated = less lower back load. Press overhead, slow and controlled on the way down. |
| Dumbbell bent-over row | 3×12 | Hinge forward, brace core hard. Pull to hip — not shoulder. Squeeze at top. |

> ⚠️ Lighten weight on RDL if back talks
