# Vera Molnár

#learn #art #design #algorithms #generative

Hungarian-French artist who spent decades making algorithmic drawings by hand before computers existed, then spent the rest of her life making them *with* computers. Died 2023 at 99.

Known for body-to-output translation and generative art before anyone had a word for it. Relevant to AIR and interaction vocabulary work.
