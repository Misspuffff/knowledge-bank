# CMF Studies

> Color / material / finish studies for the Skullcandy CANDI personas.

---

## What it is
Per-persona CMF exploration done as part of the Skullcandy CANDI project. Each study maps visual language + material treatment to one of the CANDI personas.

## Personas covered
Chiller, Fairy, Loserdom, Neon, Party Rock, Punkthorn, Skullpture, Steez, Toybox, Yolo.

## Files
Ten PNG studies — one per persona — in the source folder. File names are self-describing (`chillercmf.png`, `fairycmf.png`, `loserdomcmf.png`, `neoncmf.png`, `partyrockcmf.png`, `punkthorncmf.png`, `skullpturecmf.png`, `steezcmf.png`, `toyboxcmf.png`, `yolocmf.png`).
