# AIR — Master Brief

## What It Is

A browser-based performance instrument. The webcam is the controller. Your face and hands are the interface.

AIR translates facial expressions and hand gestures into unified real-time audio and visual output — all happening live in the browser. No modes. No menus mid-performance. The performer IS the instrument.

---

## The Problem It Solves

**The "web face" phenomenon** — in electronic music performance, performers disappear behind their laptops. The machine becomes the focus; the human body retreats. AIR inverts this. The performer's face and body become the primary expressive interface.

---

## Cultural Grounding

Rooted in DJ, electronic music, and dubstep culture. The glitch aesthetic (scan-line tears, pixel scatter, digital artifacts) is not decoration — it is culturally specific. It connects visually to electronic music's relationship with technology as a material.

---

## Success Criteria

- 60–120 second screen recording demonstrating full expressive range
- End-to-end latency under 33ms
- Feel: **nuanced**, not mechanical
- Intended use: LinkedIn/portfolio content
- Demonstrates creative direction + interaction design in an AI-assisted development context

---

## What It Is NOT

- Not a multi-instrument selector
- No drums/piano/guitar modes
- Not a utility app or productivity tool
- The visual system is core — not decorative
