# 02 — Features Copy
*App feature copy: headlines + body. Organized by feature area.*

---

## 1. Digitalizing the Closet

**Option A**
- **Header:** A closet in your pocket
- **Body:** Upload your pieces to visualize outfits and give your stylist insight.

**Option B**
- **Header:** Pieces in your pocket
- **Body:** Upload clothes from your camera roll or online to visualize outfits.

**Notes:** "A closet in your pocket" is clean but slightly generic — the *why* is missing. Explore angles around: finally using what you already own, giving your stylist something to work with.

---

## 2. Matching With a Stylist

**Option A**
- **Header:** Stylists that get you
- **Body:** Select from our variety of stylists for scheduled or on demand fashion advice.

**Option B**
- **Header:** Stylists for any budget and any body
- **Body:** Pick a stylist for scheduled or on demand fashion advice.

**Notes:** "Any budget and any body" is doing real work — inclusive, specific, not corporate. "Fashion advice" undersells it — this is *styling*, which is more personal.

---

## 3. Discover Your Style / Identity

**Option A**
- **Header:** Real looks for real people.
- **Body:** Your stylists are ready to meet any and every fashion need.

**Option B**
- **Header:** Style that spells y-o-u.
- **Body:** Get real with your stylists to discover looks that make sense for you.

**Option C**
- **Header:** Get real.
- **Body:** Feel and look confident for every occasion with your LookLine stylist.

**Notes:** "Get real." is the strongest headline — punchy, earned, on voice. "Feel and look confident for every occasion" is generic — every styling brand says this.
