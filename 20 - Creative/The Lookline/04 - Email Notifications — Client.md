# 04 — Email Notifications: Client
*All client-facing transactional emails. Brief + copy drafts in one place.*

## 1. Welcome / Account Creation

**Subject Line:** Welcome to The LookLine, [Client Name] 👠
**Preview Text:** Personal styling made sense for you
**CTAs:** Schedule a Stylist / Upload Pieces to Closet / Walk-Through The LookLine

**Purpose:** Warm welcome to new clients. Covers: feature overview, mission, pricing snapshot, CTAs to book or fill closet.
