# 01 — Brand Voice
*The canonical reference. When in doubt, come back here.*

---

## The One-Liner
> **Write like a cool older sibling — witty, warm, direct, never corporate.**

The user should feel understood, not marketed to.

---

## Tone Benchmarks

| Example | Why It Works |
|---------|-------------|
| *"Screw-top wine doesn't mean it's cheap"* | Confident, slightly provocative, instantly relatable — never stuffy |
| *"Are you guys on The Lookline?"* | Sounds like a real person saying it out loud. No brand voice, all vibe |

---

## The Four Dials

| Quality | What It Looks Like | What It Doesn't Look Like |
|---------|--------------------|--------------------------|
| **Witty** | A smart turn of phrase, a little unexpected | Trying too hard, puns for puns' sake |
| **Warm** | You're rooting for the person reading | Saccharine, over-affectionate, hollow |
| **Direct** | Get to the point fast | Hedging, padding, corporate qualifiers |
| **Never corporate** | Sounds like a human said it | Sounds like a press release |

---

## What To Never Write

❌ Elevate your wardrobe
❌ Curated just for you
❌ Your style journey
❌ Effortless style
❌ Fashion-forward
❌ Unlock your potential
❌ We're here for you
❌ Formal sign-offs of any kind
❌ Copy that could belong to any other brand

---

## Who You're Writing For

**Age:** 22–35, Gen Z to Millennial
**Headspace:** Navigating debt, career pivots, identity shifts
**Relationship to fashion:** They care, but it feels inaccessible and expensive
**What they want:** Personalization — not aspiration
**Scroll behavior:** Fast. No patience for vague, no interest in generic

---

## The Mantra

> *People want real, personalized fashion but struggle to make trends work for them. Every piece of copy should make that problem feel solved.*

---

## Color + Typography

**Colors:**
- Light pink `#F19484` → only on deep teal `#003549`
- Navy `#DFEBF0` → on light blue `#E8EFF2`
- ⚠️ Never put light pink on a light background — legibility fails

**Fonts:**
- Headlines → **Unbounded** — keep them short and punchy; they carry weight
- Body → **Montserrat** — can breathe a little more
