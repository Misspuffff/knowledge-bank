# 09 — Subscription & Promo Copy
*Stylist subscription screen copy, promo code strategy, and free trial UX.*

---

## Why the Coin Economy Exists

The Lookline uses StyleCoins to avoid Apple's **30% in-app purchase fee** on individual session payments. Instead of charging per-session, users buy coin bundles once. Fixed pricing tiers mean users know exactly how many coins to buy. Copy needs to make it feel simple, not confusing.

---

## Stylist Subscription Model

| Plan | Price | Trial |
|------|-------|-------|
| Monthly | $20.00/month | 3-day free trial |
| Yearly | $17.99/month ($214.99/year) | 7-day free trial |

Best value callout: **Save 10%** on yearly vs monthly.

**Stylist earnings:** Standard/mid-tier up to **$25 per session**. Professional tier: **set their own rate**.

## Promo Code Strategy

### Phased Rollout

**Current: Beta / Soft Launch (~10 Stylists)**
- Beta-Testers: one promo code, limited to 10
- Friends & Family: one promo code, limited to N users
- Benefit: Discount or permanently free for 3 months
- Purpose: Qualitative feedback, user-testing

**Phase 1 (Mid-May) — 50–100 Stylists**
- New / Founding Member stylists: one promo code, limited to 50–100
- Benefit: Lifetime rate (high retention value)
- Purpose: Keep conversion going while free trial model is finalized

**Phase 1.5 (Mid-June) — Open Free Trial**
- Free trial → pay. Fixed duration, a few days. For any new stylist.
- This replaces promo codes entirely — scalable model
