## Product Architecture

Two user types, two marketplaces:
- **Clients** — book through the app, pay with StyleCoins
- **Stylists** — subscribe to a tier, earn StyleCoins that cash out to real money

**StyleCoins:** In-app currency. Clients buy in bundles (avoids Apple's 30% cut). Fixed pricing tiers.

**Session types:** Hotline Request (on-demand) · Scheduled Session (booked in advance)

**Stylist tiers:** Entry → mid-tier → Professional (Pro stylists set their own pricing)

## Quick Reminders

- Write like a **cool older sibling** — not a brand manager
- Never: *elevate, curate, journey, effortless style*
- Default: **3 variations**, meaningfully distinct
- Palette: light pink `#F19484` only on deep teal `#003549`
- **In app UI:** pink = destructive only. Navy = primary CTA. Soft gray = secondary CTA.
- Headlines → **Unbounded** (short + punchy). Body → **Montserrat**
- Register: *"Are you guys on The Lookline?"*
- **Content drives layout** — Juan's rule. Finalize copy before design locks.
