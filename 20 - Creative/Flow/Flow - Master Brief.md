# Flow — Master Brief

> *"Color flows like sound."*

## What Is Flow?

Flow (IdeationBot) is a **generative ideation assistant** — a hybrid between a brainstorming partner and a creative thinking tool. It lives as a web-based interface connecting to Gemini for large-language reasoning and multimodal input.

**The goal:** help designers move through a structured yet fluid sequence:
1. Vague idea
2. Structured creative concept
3. Tangible output (product spec, prompt, storyboard, or visual moodboard)

---

## The Ideation Sequence

| Stage | Name | What Happens |
|-------|------|-------------|
| 1 | **Inspiration Input** | Upload images, text, or sketches |
| 2 | **Associative Expansion** | Gemini proposes metaphors, visual patterns, design themes |
| 3 | **Constraint Reframing** | Constraints become creative catalysts, not blockers |
| 4 | **Remix & Reframe** | Divergent versions — aesthetic, functional, narrative, emotional |
| 5 | **Synthesis & Output** | Concept cards, prompts, or slide-ready design documentation |

---

## Core Philosophy

Flow is meant to feel like a **creative studio in motion** — visually mirroring how inspiration moves.

- **Metaphor:** flow as an energy field; nodes and connections pulse to represent thought branching
- **HCI Principles:** calm technology, minimal input friction, real-time feedback loops
- **Design Theme:** synesthetic gradient — *color flows like sound*

It should bridge **intuition and structured reasoning**, letting designers externalize their thought process and re-enter it later like a living sketchbook.

---

## Long-Term Vision

Flow evolves toward an **adaptive ideation ecosystem** that can:

- Learn your creative rhythm (pace, word choice, energy patterns)
- Adjust tone and level of abstraction dynamically
- Serve as both mentor and mirror during creative work

---

## Differentiation

| Traditional Brainstorming Tools | Flow |
|--------------------------------|------|
| Collect ideas | Interprets relationships between ideas |
| Static outputs | Maps conceptual topology |
| Single modality | Multimodal (text + image + color) |
| You manage structure | Structure emerges from the session |
