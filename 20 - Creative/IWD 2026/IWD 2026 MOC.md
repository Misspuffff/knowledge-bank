# IWD 2026 — Map of Content

> *"Design that doesn't take a position isn't neutral — it's complicit."*

A research-driven editorial web experience built for Lovable's International Women's Day 2026 community event. The piece investigates the gender gap across design, AI, and engineering — grounded in data, executed in a visual language borrowed from protest print culture.

First time using Lovable as a build platform.

## Pipeline Established

2. **Iterative brief development** → Claude (multiple rounds before touching a build tool)
3. **Build** → Lovable

The brief-first discipline was the key move — going into Lovable with a fully resolved concept produced a stronger outcome than building speculatively.
